How does our technology work?

We deliver the best of both worlds - a combination of both software and hardware.

World-Class Software

Intuitive Wearables

  • Patients can easily consult with a doctor of their choice in a video call format.
  • Doctors have access to all the tools from a regular clinic like patient vitals and medical history.
  • Our proprietary electronic technology ensures the data is effectively relayed to your doctor
  • Simply connecting it to a computer gives the doctor great insight into the patient's physiological state.

We deliver a full end-to-end patient and doctor experience, that completely eliminates the need to leave your home for a check-up.

1

Put on the Medvita wearable

The Medvita wearable is safely fitted with all the sensors that your doctor would normally have access to at the clinic. By wearing it, your doctor can read and see the vital information needed to give helpful medical advice.

2

Log-in to Medvita - Patient

When logging onto Medvita - Patient, you are quickly brought to a modern and easy-to-use user interface that will allow you to book appointments with a licensed medical professional.

3

Join the call and connect with your physician

When it's time for your appointment, log on and in minutes, you will be in a video conference with your doctor. It's as simple as that!
